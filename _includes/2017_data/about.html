What is CultureX?

The CultureX event is a collaborative effort of various organizations to promote and celebrate our diverse and multicultural campus!

CultureX 2020 is the 1st annual CultureX at the University of Manitoba.

When and Where is CultureX?

CultureX 2020 will be celebrated over 2 days. On March 20th a variety things like Face Painting, Flag Parade, Henna Tattoos, A photobooth and a DJ will be in University Center.

On March 22, there will be an Evening of Diversity from 5:30-9:00PM in the Multipurpose Room at University Center.

What is the Evening of Diversity?

The Evening of Diversity is a formal dinner which will consist of perfomances, a fashion show and special guests from the community.

We encourage everyone to wear Traditional Clothing from their culture.

Who can attend and Is the event Free?

The celebrations on March 20th are open for everyone. The Evening of Diversity tickets are $15 till March 14 and $20 after that for any remaining spots. Students, faculty and family are all welcome to attend.

How do i get a ticket?

You can reach out to any of the collaborating students groups for tickets. There will also be few tickets available at the UMSU Service Center.

Can I re-sell my ticket?

It is forbidden to re-sell the tickets above the purchased price. If you cannot attend anymore, reach out to us and we will do our best to assist you.
