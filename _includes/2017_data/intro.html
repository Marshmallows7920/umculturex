An Intercultural celebration


The heart of this event is to promote collaboration and integration across the wider international community at the University of Manitoba. Over the last few years, the university has experienced an expansion and increase in the number of international students, but there still exists a wide gap between how these students interact with the resources available to support them and with their immediate community and its members.

By having an event like this, we hope to increase an awareness, respect and sensitivity among international students; for other cultures, religions and people.
